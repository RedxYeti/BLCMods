Hello there, I wanted to put together a rebalance for the best boi, DeathTrap
Primarily, I felt that he could use some love in regards to his skills (especially ones that aren't Robot rampage), non-melee scaling, and his AI
with the goal of him being more useful in UVHM, and if invested in heavily, a viable playstyle

A summary of notable changes:

-Added 'general' damage scaling for DeathTrap to his existing melee damage boost skills 
-Upshot Robot now additionally heals DeathTrap for a significant amount
-Updated targetting minimum distances for Explosive Clap & The Stare
-Added Damage reductions to various DeathTrap skills

-Updated DeathTrap's AI to melee non-phaselockable enemies (most non-flying bosses & large enemies), as well as target finding for his melee attacks
  -- The targetting change has a side effect that he can no longer melee things through walls

-Added functionality to Make it Sparkle to apply to non-melee damage as well
  -- Fixed an inherent bug with Make it Sparkle that made Roid shields add to DeathTrap's level scaling for Make it Sparkle's damage

-Updated One Two Boom:
  -- Increased Damage over time applied by the explosion
  -- Increased the activation animation speed, as well as reduced the idle time after activation
  -- Allowed the projectile to self-detonate on collision with damageable environmental objects & on hitting enemies
  -- Increased the projectile speed
