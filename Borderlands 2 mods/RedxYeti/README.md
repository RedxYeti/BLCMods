This mod adds many missing legendary items to the world pools, optionally adding uniques and pearls.

This includes adding items such as chubby only items into the world pools. It does not remove any items from any vanilla pools.

It also updates the world drop values to the Blassic values, which mimics the drop rates of Borderlands 1. 
Huge thanks to Aaron0000 for letting me use those from Blassic. https://www.nexusmods.com/borderlands2/mods/195


To save on making too many categories, items like seraph items have been put in the unique options because of the many ways they're obtained.

Weapons options come with "legendaries only", "legendaries and uniques", or "legendaries, uniques and pearls".

Grenade options are "legendaries only" or "legendaries and uniques".

Artifacts are just added in to the rarest artifact pool

Shields were added to their correct corresponding world pool, however a few shield types didn't have world pools so they got added to the "Standard" shield world pool.
